Simple Omelette Recipe

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook until set.
  4. Add fillings of your choice, fold the omelette, and serve.

Nutrition

Calories: 250 | Protein: 12g | Fat: 20g | Carbs: 2g
